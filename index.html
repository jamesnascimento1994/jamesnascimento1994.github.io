About Me

I’m a musician who also found joy in software development.


I love playing music and it is still a passion of mine, but due to the current global pandemic, it is even harder to make a living while being a musician full-time. I was introduced to the idea of taking a coding bootcamp. At first I wasn’t sure if this is what I wanted to do, so I took a part time course that covered front end web development. While I was going through the online tutorials prior, I had a feeling that I would enjoy this, a lot.


About mid-way through the course, I realized I wanted to pursue this as a job. So I took a full time software engineering bootcamp. During it, I created three applications by myself and one application with three other students in my cohort. I learned so much and met very cool people. I now look at coding as a skill that I want to hone continuously. This is just the beginning.
